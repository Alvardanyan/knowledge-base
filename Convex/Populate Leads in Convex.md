# Populate Leads in Convex

## Overview

Populate Leads saves you time by automatically identifying prospect contacts for properties or
companies, allowing you to consolidate prospecting data into a single workflow. This also lets
you more accurately measure Return on Investment (ROI).

## Who uses this feature

* Managers, regional managers, representatives, and salespeople
* Primarily for Commercial Service and Commercial Construction business types

## Populate Leads

You can identify contacts while creating a target list or after the list is created. To populate the
contacts after the list is created:

1. Go to the Navigation panel > Targets.

2. Select a target list and click to open it.

3. From the table, select the targets you want to populate with contacts, or click Select All to
include all contacts in the selection.

4. Click Populate Leads.

5. On the Populate Leads pop-up that opens, select the criteria for the leads.

6. When finished, click Proceed.

## Populate Leads pop-up overview

Use the criteria in Populate Leads to customize which contacts are added to your targets.

1. On the first criteria row, select:

   a. Contact info or Job Title to populate the type of information you want for the targets.

   The criteria for the information type are as follows:

   i. Includes any

   ii. Includes all

   iii. Exclude

   b. The type of information to match the Contact info (Email, phone number, etc) and Job
   title (Facility Manager, Owner, etc.)

> **Tip:** You can create and add your own job titles in addition to the ones that are
displayed. Type the job title in the area and click it.

2. Click +Add Row to add a new row.

> **Note:** Click -(icon) to remove the row.

3. From the Include contacts within dropdown, select one of the following options to reduce
or expand the area that is searched for contacts:

   a. sites

   b. states

   c. nation

4. From the Include up to ... contact(s) per target dropdown, select one of the following
options to populate the number of contacts for each target:

   a. 1

   b. 3

   c. 5
