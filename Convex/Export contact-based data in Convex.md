# Export contact-based data in Convex

## Overview

Use contact-based export to pull detailed information about people and contacts for properties, making it easier to target outreach and plan territories. It provides up-to-date contact lists for cold calls, emails, and in-person visits, helping optimize campaigns, track engagement, and ensure teams work with accurate, actionable data.

## Who uses this feature

* Managers, regional managers, representatives, and salespeople
* Primarily for Commercial Service and Commercial Construction business types

## Feature configuration

* Contact [marketing@convexlabs.io](mailto:marketing@convexlabs.io) to set up a demo and get more information about Convex.

You can export the contact-based data in two ways:

- From the Maps feature section (also called people-based export).
- From the Targets feature section.

## People-based export

1. Go to Map and click Filters.

2. From the Contact Attributes filter, select any filter and click Apply.

> **Tip:** Use more filters or zoom in to have a manageable number of contacts.

3. When you are satisfied with the results, click View Contacts.

![Alt text](https://github.com/Alvardanyan/knowledge-base-portfolio/blob/94105de997c43eadf5a010cbcd34455f45541ebd/images/export-contact-based-data-in-convex-image-uuedwrnw.jpg)

4. From the People screen that opens, click Export.

5. In the Export Contacts pop-up that opens, click Continue.

> **Note:** Employees with export permissions receive a limited number of export credits each month. Every contact that is part of the export consumes a credit. However, after a contact has been exported, that same contact can be exported again without taking from the credit pool for an entire year. Contact your CSM to set up contact exports for your users.

6. You'll receive an email with a download link from support@convexlabs.io. Click the link to start the download.

> **Note:** The link expires in 24 hours.

## Contact-based export from Targets

1. Go to Targets and click the target list you want to export.

2. On the target list screen that opens, click Export.

![Alt text](https://github.com/Alvardanyan/knowledge-base-portfolio/blob/2926362fbb2dd514e1d3a761f874c6d05d1f4fa2/images/export-contact-based-data-in-convex-image-v0gzbo3b.jpg)

3. In the Export pop-up that opens, click Export.

## Export pop-up description

The Export pop-up includes information on:

- The number of targets that are included in the export request.
- The number of contacts that are included in those targets.
- The total amount of credits assigned to the user per month.
- The total number of credits used so far this month.
- The number of credits required for this export.

> **Note:** The number might be different from the total contacts in the request if a portion of the contacts are duplicates from previous requests.

- The number of credits that would remain if requesting the export.
