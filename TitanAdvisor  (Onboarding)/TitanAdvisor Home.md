# TitanAdvisor Home

TitanAdvisor guides you through your journey to ServiceTitan mastery, letting you know what you need to do every step of the way to be successful and to advance to the next stage. It's an automated best practice guide that helps you get the most out of ServiceTitan — making personalized recommendations for features your organization can implement to achieve your business goals.


## Setup Checklist

### TitanAdvisor Prepare to Launch

While your company prepares to go live, use Prepare to Launch to set up required features and complete required training for a successful launch.

### TitanAdvisor administrator overview

After going live, administrators access important metrics about how your organization uses ServiceTitan and tracks employee post-launch training progress.

### TitanAdvisor Training View for office employees

Non-administrator office employees can access the Training View to see their training progress and access training courses.

### Included with ServiceTitan

TitanAdvisor is available to all ServiceTitan customers as a core feature — no additional subscription required.

### Scoring & Stages

TitanAdvisor tracks your progress through stages, scoring your feature adoption and training completion as you advance.

## Frequently Asked Questions

#### What is TitanAdvisor and who can access it?

TitanAdvisor is an automated best practice guide included with ServiceTitan. Administrators have access to the full administrative view with metrics and team training progress. Non-administrator office employees can access the Training View to track their own progress and complete training courses.

#### What is TitanAdvisor Prepare to Launch?

While your company is preparing to go live with ServiceTitan, you have access to TitanAdvisor Prepare to Launch. It guides you through setting up required features and completing required training in preparation for a successful launch.
