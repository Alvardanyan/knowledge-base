# Duplicate Service Agreements

## Overview

The Duplicate Agreement feature enables you to create a new Service Agreement (SA) by cloning the existing SA. This saves time to create similar agreements by minimizing manual re-entries and errors, and streamlines workflows by duplicating agreement details into a new draft.

Duplicate an agreement from:

- SA Proposal Builder screen when it's in the Draft status.
- Service Agreement Dashboard when it's in the Activated status.

## Things to know

- You can duplicate Service Agreements only within a single customer.
- Duplicating an agreement creates a new agreement in Draft status, regardless of the status of the original agreement.

## Who uses this feature

- Administrators, accountants, estimators, project managers, and operations managers
- Primarily benefits Commercial Service and Replacement and Commercial Construction business types
- Applies to all trades

## Duplicate Agreements from SA Proposal Builder screen

1. On the Create a service agreement step 7: Preview and Send screen, click Duplicate Agreement.

2. On the Duplicate Agreement pop-up that opens:

   a. Enter the Agreement Name.

   b. Select a Service Agreement Template.

   c. Select a Business Unit.

3. When finished, click Duplicate.

The duplicated agreement is created in the Draft status.

## Duplicate Agreements from SA Dashboard

1. Open the activated Service Agreement.

2. From the SA Dashboard, click More, and then select Duplicate Agreement.

3. On the Duplicate Agreement pop-up that opens:

   a. Enter the Agreement Name.

   b. Select a Service Agreement Template.

   c. Select a Business Unit.

4. When finished, click Duplicate.

The duplicated agreement is created in the Draft status.
